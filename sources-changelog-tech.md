# Tech Sources — Changelog

This file logs how the **Tech** source roster (`sources-tech.json`) evolves over time. The daily 6 AM briefing task vets general-technology outlets (big tech & business, devices & consumer products, software & platforms, science & emerging tech), adds up to 3 newly qualifying sources per day (deduped by domain), and records every change here. Seeded core sources are never removed. This roster is a sibling to the Cyber and AI briefings and deliberately avoids narrow cybersecurity and pure AI-model coverage.

## 2026-06-17 — Seed roster established

Seeded the initial Tech roster with **16 sources** across four categories:

- **Big Tech & Business (7):** The Verge, TechCrunch, Ars Technica, Bloomberg Technology, Reuters Technology, CNBC Technology, The Information, plus the Stratechery newsletter.
- **Devices & Consumer (4):** Engadget, The Gadgeteer, 9to5Mac, Tom's Hardware.
- **Software & Platforms (2):** The Register, ZDNet.
- **Science & Emerging (2):** Ars Technica Science, Space.com.

Tiers: 7 core, 8 strong, 1 newsletter. These 16 seeded sources form the baseline roster; the daily task will append new vetted sources below as they are discovered.
