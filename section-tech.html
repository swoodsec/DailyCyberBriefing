Google opens Android to third-party app stores today

📅 Jul 15, 2026

As of today, July 22, US developers' app and game listings become available to enrolled third-party Android app stores directly through Google Play, with downloads still processed via Play on the same terms as a direct install. The change stems from the long-running Google v. Epic Games court order.

Why it matters: Making rival storefronts reachable a tap away inside Play is a meaningful crack in Android's distribution moat, and a template regulators may point to as they push Apple toward similar openness.

SpaceX launches Northrop Grumman's satellite-repair robot

📅 Jul 21, 2026

A Falcon 9 launched Northrop Grumman's Mission Robotic Vehicle and three Mission Extension Pods from Cape Canaveral. The 3,000 kg MRV, fitted with two 10-foot robotic arms, will spend a year cruising to geosynchronous orbit before docking fuel-carrying pods onto aging satellites owned by Optus and SES, extending each by up to eight years.

Why it matters: It's one of the most ambitious commercial satellite-servicing missions flown to date, and a real test of whether robotic repair in orbit can become routine rather than experimental.
