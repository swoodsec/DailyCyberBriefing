Webb uncovers a hidden third planet in the Beta Pictoris system

📅 Jul 22, 2026

Using the NIRSpec integral field unit, astronomers detected Beta Pictoris d not as a point of light but by the chemical fingerprint of its atmosphere — carbon monoxide, water vapor, and methane — making Beta Pictoris only the second system with at least three directly imaged planets. The result is published in Astrophysical Journal Letters.

Why it matters: Finding a planet by its atmospheric signature rather than its glow is a new detection technique that could reveal fainter, dust-obscured worlds around other stars.
NASA Science

📅 Jul 22, 2026

HD Hyundai Heavy Industries said assembly of the final vacuum vessel sector module for ITER, the international fusion megaproject in southern France, is complete — the firm produced four of the nine sectors that will contain the reactor's superheated plasma.

Why it matters: Finishing the vacuum vessel is a key hardware step toward first plasma at the world's largest fusion experiment, whose results will inform whether magnetic-confinement fusion can ever supply grid power.
