📅 Jul 13, 2026

A joint advisory says hackers tied to Russia's FSB Center 16 — tracked as Berzerk Bear or Dragonfly — are sending SNMP set-requests to poorly secured, often end-of-life Cisco Smart Install devices, copying their configurations to attacker-controlled servers via TFTP. Why it matters: This is the same FSB unit the EU and UK just named and sanctioned — a reminder that publicly attributing an actor doesn't stop the campaign; unpatched, forgotten network gear remains its preferred way in.
